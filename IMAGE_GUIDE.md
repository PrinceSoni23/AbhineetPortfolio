### 1. Hero Section Images (3 landscape backgrounds)

**File Names:**

- `chemistry-lab-1.jpg`
- `chemistry-lab-2.jpg`
- `chemistry-lab-3.jpg`

**Recommended Specifications:**

- **Size**: 1920x1080px (Full HD landscape)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Content**: Chemistry laboratory scenes, equipment, research environments
- **Style**: Professional, well-lit, high quality

**Where to find:**

- Unsplash.com - Search: "chemistry lab", "laboratory", "science research"
- Pexels.com - Search: "chemistry", "laboratory equipment"
- Your own lab photos

### 2. Professor Portrait (Hero Section)

**File Name:**

- `professor.jpg`

**Recommended Specifications:**

- **Size**: 800x1000px (Portrait orientation)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Content**: Professional headshot or full-body photo of the professor
- **Background**: Preferably plain or blurred (easy to cut out)
- **Style**: Professional attire, good lighting

### 3. Professor Portrait (About Section)

## ðŸŽ¨ Tips for Best Results

### Image Quality

- Use high-resolution images (at least the sizes mentioned above)
- Avoid blurry or pixelated photos
- Ensure good lighting in all photos

### Consistency

- Try to maintain a consistent color tone across images
- Professional and academic look
- Clean, uncluttered backgrounds

### File Size Optimization

- Compress images to reduce file size while maintaining quality
- Tools: TinyPNG.com, Squoosh.app
- Target: Under 500KB per image

## ðŸ”„ How to Add Images

1. **Create the folder** (if not exists):

   - Navigate to `public/` folder
   - Create a new folder named `images`

2. **Add your images**:

   - Copy all images to `public/images/` folder
   - Ensure file names match exactly (case-sensitive)

3. **Verify**:
   - Check that all 12 images are present
   - Verify file names are correct
   - Run the dev server to see your images
