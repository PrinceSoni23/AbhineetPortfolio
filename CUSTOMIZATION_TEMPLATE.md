## üí° Writing Tips

### For Descriptions:

- Keep descriptions to 2-3 sentences
- Focus on impact and significance
- Use active voice
- Be specific but concise

### For Titles:

- Be descriptive but not too long
- Use professional terminology
- Highlight the key focus

### For Impact Statements:

Examples:

- "High Impact Factor"
- "Breakthrough Research"
- "International Recognition"
- "National Honor"
- "Environmental Impact"
- "Clinical Applications"
- "Innovative Methodology"
