### Customization Tips:

**If you want to add subtle color:**

- Add a single accent color (e.g., muted blue: `#3b82f6`)
- Use it sparingly for CTA buttons or highlights
- Keep 90% of the design in grays/whites

**For a warmer feel:**

- Change gray-900 to a warm charcoal: `#2d3748`
- Add slight warm tints to backgrounds

**For more contrast:**

- Use pure black (`#000000`) instead of gray-900
- Increase border weights to 2px
